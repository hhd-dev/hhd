    How to Dump Your Original Xbox Discs for Emulation

    1. You must own the original Xbox game disc.
    2. Use a compatible DVD drive with your PC (not all drives work with Xbox discs).
    3. Download the extract-xiso tool or similar to create an .iso or .xiso file.
    4. Follow the instructions to dump the disc and save the ISO to your PC.
    5. Transfer the ISO to your Android device and load it in the emulator.
    6. Never download ISOs you do not own. This is illegal and against emulator policy.

    For more detailed guides, see xemu's official documentation.

    Can I play Xbox Game Pass games in this emulator?
